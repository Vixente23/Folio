About Me.

Passionate about technology and design, I am a web developer, mobile developer, and UX/UI designer. With one year of experience, I’ve had the opportunity to work on various projects that have strengthened my technical skills while developing a keen eye for detail and user experience. Independent, curious, and always looking for new challenges, I am proficient in modern web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, etc.) as well as mobile development tools (such as React Native or Flutter). At the same time, my design sensibility allows me to create interfaces that are both visually appealing and user-friendly.

My goal is to build high-performance websites and applications that are enjoyable to use and tailored to real user needs. I place great importance on clean code, accessibility, and optimizing the user experience. I’m constantly learning, experimenting with new ideas, and improving my skills to deliver the best in every project.
